How It Works

  • 1

    Log Collection & Normalize

    We ingest heterogeneous logs (Auth, System, Network). All timestamps are synchronized, and varying formats are normalized into a standard JSON schema.

  • 2

    Dimensionality Reduction

    High-dimensional log data is compressed using PCA (Principal Component Analysis) to retain only the most variant signals, reducing noise.

  • 3

    Statistical Training

    The Isolation Forest model trains on the clean historical data, building a decision tree forest that isolates data points. The easier a point is to isolate, the more anomalous it is.

  • 4

    Real-time Scoring

    As live logs flow in, they are passed through the forest hierarchy. Each event receives a precise mathematical anomaly score.

  • 5

    Explanation & Alerting

    High-score events are enriched with context (Who, Where, What) and sent to the dashboard. The system explains why the score is high.
